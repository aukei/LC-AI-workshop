# AI Assistant Access for Developers
## Business Case for Unrestricted Access

**Recommendation:** Provide free access to AI assistants (Grok, Gemini, etc.) for all 33 developers & sysadmins

---

## Quantifiable ROI: 8-10x Return on Investment

### Current State
- **Monthly AI spend:** $1,270 (825M tokens via OpenRouter)
- **Cost per developer:** $38/month
- **Critical issue:** 50% of developer time spent on infrastructure troubleshooting vs actual development

### Projected Impact
- **25-30% reduction in troubleshooting time** = 20 hours/month saved per developer
- **Monthly value:** 20 hrs × $16/hr × 33 devs = **$10,560 saved**
- **Net ROI:** $10,560 ÷ $1,270 = **8.3x monthly return**
- **Annual impact:** ~$127,000 value creation vs $15,000 cost

*Industry benchmark: GitHub Copilot users show 55% faster task completion (McKinsey 2023)*
